SD-Steel is proud to introduce our Flow Rate Adjustment Stainless Steel Capillary Tubes. Our Flow Rate Adjustment Stainless Steel Capillary Tubes are manufactured using premium-grade 316L stainless steel. This material offers superior corrosion resistance, making it suitable for use with a wide range of fluids, including aggressive chemicals and saline solutions. The high tensile strength and excellent mechanical properties of 316L stainless steel ensure that these tubes can withstand the rigors of continuous use without compromising performance.
